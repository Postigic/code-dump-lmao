Clown fish is the name of several types of fish that live in tropical oceans

Clownfish Physical Features

Clown fish can grow to a length of about 4 inches (10 centimeters).
Females are larger than males. Most types of clown fish have bright white stripes on a colorful body



Two of the best-known types are the true clown fish and the false clown fish.
They look nearly the same. The true clown fish is also known as the Eastern clown fish.
The false clown fish is also known as the Western or common clown fish.
The main character of the animated movie Finding Nemo (2003) is a false clown fish.
